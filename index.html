ABOUT US

KRI8 /krēˈāt/

KRI8 is a platform for customers to buy or rent tools, rent co-working space, and learn how to use the tools by our tutorials videos. Not only that, KRI8 also provide amenities for users to sell their handmade products.

As product designer students, we are aware that tools have a huge role in creating a product. Our goal is to provide and help people with limited tools/ designer student to make their imaginations a reality. Nevertheless, a right and comfortable space is also important. Here we have a solution by providing a co-working space accommodated with tools and an instructor that will guide you!

GOOD NEWS!
You can use our facilities anywhere in Indonesia. Our workshop and co-working space are spanned across the country, available in Jakarta, Bali, Yogyakarta, Surabaya, Medan, Balikpapan, etc. Shippings are available across the country too!
